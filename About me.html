My hobby

Somehow, I prepared for the competition three years in a row, and as I received more and more good awards, I found it very interesting to prepare for the competition. The photos below are the results of three competitions. I moved when I was in 5th grade, so I quit piano academy. Therefore, I haven't been able to prepare for the competition since then. Although I couldn't run toward my dream of becoming a pianist, these experiences are precious in that I can enjoy piano as a hobby now.

Among swimming types, I liked breaststroke the most and was confident. When I was a senior in elementary school, I won 3rd place in the local swimming competition. I was an elementary school swimmer. Now that I think about it, it's a very valuable experience. Even now, when I have time, I frequently go swimming pool. When I swim, I don't have any trivial thoughts and I feel at ease.
