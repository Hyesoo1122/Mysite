Character [Eeyore]



My favourite character is Eeyore . Eeyore is a donkey in Winnie the Pooh. When I was six years old, my father bought me this character pillow for Christmas present. From then on, I started to have an affection for the character Eeyore, and I really like it until now. When I see the products made of Eeyore, I always buy them and collect, so there are about 20 items related to Eeyore. Eeyore is always a gloomy and silent character. When I watch the pooh movie, I want to comfort and help him. Maybe that's why I'm more attached to it, although it's not a real animal.
